## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Ituri** Province (French: Province de l'Ituri; Swahili: Jimbo la Ituri) is one of the 26 provinces 
> of the Democratic Republic of the Congo created in the 2015 repartitioning. 
> 
> Ituri, Bas-Uele, Haut-Uele, and Tshopo provinces 
> are the result of the subdividing of the former Orientale province. 
> 
> Ituri was formed from the Ituri district 
> whose town of Bunia was elevated to capital city of the new province.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ituri%20Province)
